About OLPA

Who We Are

The “Out of Last Place Alliance” is a partnership of business leaders dedicated to getting Mississippi permanently and forever “out of last place” as defined by our state’s per-capita income, which we believe is the best economic measure of human prosperity and well-being.

Our Vision

Our vision is for Mississippi to no longer be the “poorest state in the nation,” so that no other state can legitimately still say “Thank God for Mississippi” (Wikipedia).

Our Mission

To publish comprehensive research and solutions to definitively determine:

  • When and how Mississippi got in last place
  • Why Mississippi is still in last place.
  • What specific, concrete, and actionable steps would be required to get Mississippi out of last place.
NOTE: All research and data will be published online, along with source spreadsheets and documentation, in addition to being professionally published in formal reports.
